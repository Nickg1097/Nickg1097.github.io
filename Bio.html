Bio

Danny DeVito is an American Actor who was born in New Jersey in 1944. DeVito is known for many roles such as The Penguin in Batman returns as well as doing voiceovers for animated movies such as Space Jam and The Lorax. Devito arguably is most known for his role as Frank in the hit sitcom It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia Devito has also done work starring in commercials, most recently Devito was seen during a Superbowl commercial where he was the red M&M bathing in a pool of chocolate.
